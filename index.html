Here's a Little About me

Hello there! My name is Yuke, and I am an adventurous software development engineer who has a deep love for baking, hiking, and animals. As a problem-solver, I enjoy using my coding skills to develop innovative solutions that can simplify people's lives. When I am not immersed in coding, I like to experiment with new recipes in the kitchen or head out to explore the great outdoors with my furry friends. With a curious and active nature, I am always on the lookout for fresh experiences and challenges to push my limits.

But there's something else that's really important to me: believe that every girl should have access to quality education, and I am committed to making that a reality. That's why I'm passionate about using virtual and augmented reality technology to provide girls all over the world with the tools they need to learn and thrive. Through my work in this field, I hope to inspire more women to pursue careers in STEM and to help close the gender gap in tech.

I am excited to connect with others who share my interests and values, and I am always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow.
So if you're interested in chatting about AR/VR, software development, baking, hiking, animals, or education, please don't hesitate to reach out!
